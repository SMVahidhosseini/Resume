About

Seyed Mohammad Vahidhosseini received his Ph.D. degree in mechanical engineering, energy conversion division, from Ferdowsi University of Mashhad (FUM). He received his B.Sc., 2012, and M.Sc., 2014, in mechanical engineering department at the FUM. To date, he has published six articles in peer reviewed international journals. He worked in the heat transfer laboratory of the FUM as a teaching fellow for six semesters. He worked on a porous radiant burner with internal combustion regime and convective drying as the Ph.D. thesis and Master thesis, respectively. After his Ph.D., he has started learning web programming and web development. In this field, he has learned Javascript frameworks of React, React Native, Next, and Svelte.
  • His web-based work interest is to design and develop websites using the server-side rendering (SSR) and static site generation (SSG) by the Next.js framework.
  • His research interests in the mechanical engineering include the premixed combustion in porous radiant burners, convective drying, Green’s Function Method (GFM), data science, local entropy generation analysis, mathematical modelling, and the analytical and exact solutions of mechanical problems.
